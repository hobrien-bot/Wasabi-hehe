Amazing Facts

  • Real wasabi is one of the most expensive vegetables in the world.
  • Fresh wasabi loses flavour quickly after being grated.
  • Most wasabi outside Japan is actually fake.
  • Wasabi belongs to the cabbage family.
  • Its spicy effect mainly targets the nose.
  • Wasabi plants can take years to grow.
  • Scientists tested wasabi for emergency alarm systems.
  • Wasabi grows naturally near streams.
  • Fresh wasabi tastes sweeter than imitation wasabi.

Fun Trivia

Some people compare the feeling of eating wasabi to breathing spicy air.

Wasabi flavour is now used in:

  • Potato chips
  • Peanuts
  • Crackers
  • Instant noodles
  • Mayonnaise
  • Popcorn
