Wasabi Mayo

Mix mayonnaise with a small amount of wasabi paste for a spicy dipping sauce.

This works well with fries, burgers, sushi, and seafood.

Wasabi Sushi

Traditional sushi often includes wasabi between the rice and fish.

Chefs use only a small amount because real wasabi has a strong flavour.

Wasabi Peas

Crunchy wasabi peas are a popular snack around the world.

They combine spicy wasabi flavour with roasted peas for a crunchy texture.

Creative Foods

Modern chefs now use wasabi in:

  • Ice cream
  • Chocolate
  • Steak sauces
  • Dressings
  • Popcorn flavouring
