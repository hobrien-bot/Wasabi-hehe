Why Does Wasabi Burn?

Wasabi contains chemicals called isothiocyanates. These chemicals travel into the nose and stimulate nerve endings.

This causes the famous sharp burning feeling that quickly disappears.

Unlike chilli peppers, wasabi does not mainly affect the tongue.

Health Benefits

  • Contains antioxidants
  • May help fight bacteria
  • Low calorie food
  • Contains vitamins and minerals
  • Can improve flavour without adding much fat

Modern Research

Scientists continue studying wasabi because some compounds may help health and food safety.

Research has looked at:

  • Anti-bacterial properties
  • Food preservation
  • Health compounds
  • Medical technology ideas
